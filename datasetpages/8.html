Bike Lanes, Signed Routes & Multi-use Trails

Custodian

Transportation Planning and Parks & Facilities, posted by the Geomatics Division

Data Currency Comments

The data was posted on Sept 1, 2020 and will be updated as required.

Dataset Description

These files are created from polylines and contain Type- bike lanes, signed routes and multi-use trails. Also the name of the Trails, if named and the length, if provided.

Data Accuracy Comments

This linework is maintained by Transportation Planning and Parks & Facilities and updated using a variety of resources, including information from the Geomatics Division.

Attributes

The attributes include the Type- bike lanes, signed routes or multi-use trails. The name of the Trails if named and the length if provided.
